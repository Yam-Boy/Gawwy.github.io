My Personal Development Journey

The last five years of my life consists of me entering 4th form of my high school, Bridgeport high,
to the continuation of my university life as a second year student at The University of Technology.
During my adolescent years I spent a lot of time contemplating life and ruminating on the thought
of self-improvement, though this was more so in an effort to be acknowledged by my peers rather
than any benefits I would expereince after reaping the fruits of my labor, labors which included.

  • Reading
  • Meditation
  • Creating Art
  • Routine Excercise
This period of improving myself allowed me to develop my self-discipline to higher degree than it
was previously. Secondly my creativity as an individual was also bolstered due to the struggle of
having to create new interesting pieces of art consistently. Finally, the amount new content that I
was reading increased my vocabulary, comprehensive ability, developed my critical thinking skills
and decision-making skills. With all this in consideration.I can confidently say
that my strengths are:
  • My Intellect
  • My Discipline
  • My Creativity
